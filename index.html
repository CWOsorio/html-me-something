DIY Crafting

A Beautiful World of Creation

DIY crafting is an activity that I have always loved even before I started doing it myself. I've always enjoyed looking at the creations that other crafters have come up with. I always enjoyed going to craft shows or watching any type of DIY show on television. Eventually I found myself saying more often "hey, I can do that"! So, I did!

My kiddos had the pleasure of being my first "models". I would design shirts for them all of the time. It was really exciting when they starting coming up with their own ideas. They would be elated when their shirts were completed with thier ideas. My daughter came up with the wording for her last day of kindergarten shirt. All of the verbiage that we would see for end of the school year, had a bit of a "good riddance" tone to it and that didn't fit her true feels. She loved everything about kindergarten and was sad to leave. Here she is wearing her shirt with her own words.

Then I started with paper crafting and making cute little shadow boxes with rolled paper flowers inside.

From there I transistioned to creating hair bows for my daughter and making fun personalized shirts for them.

My latest creations are all about sparkle and shine! It's a tideous yet relaxing process for a beautiful outcome. I started small by in corporating rhinestones into the center of the paper flowers that I would make and now my goal is to complete and entire cup with rhinestones. My first completed bling project popsocket.

I'm excited about expanding my skills and can't wait to complete my first cup.

There are so many crafters that I follow that have inspired my ideas. Since I have been into sparkle lately I have been following a crafter who calls herself the Crystal Ninja. Her work is impecable. She is an award winning artist who has spread sparkle all over the world.

Quick bio on who the Crystal Ninja is!

The Crystal Ninja has been bringing bling to the world for over 15 years! Her work is unmatched and can been seen on the runway and several popular logos.
